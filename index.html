About Me

I'm Matt, a public health practitioner, ceramic artist, and writer based in Portland, Oregon. My work bridges the worlds of housing, health equity, and public policy. I’m currently conducting a Fulbright research fellowship at National Taiwan University, in Taipei, Taiwan, exploring how home environments shape health — and how local communities and traditions influence housing design and environmental policy.

With a background in U.S. housing policy and grassroots public health, I’m interested in how space, culture, and history intersect in everyday life. This site is a place to document research, field notes, and reflections from my time in Taiwan.

This site documents a Fulbright research project on healthy housing and environmental health in Taiwan, integrating field interviews, local perspectives, and visual documentation to explore the relationship between home, health, and place.

本网站记录了一项在台湾进行的富布赖特研究项目，聚焦于健康住房和环境健康。通过实地访谈、地方视角和视觉资料，本项目探讨居住环境、健康与地域之间的关系。
